  • Data Protection

    Ensuring the privacy of individuals whose data is processed is not only a legal obligation, but above all a matter of respecting fundamental rights.

    Implementing an efficient Data Protection Management System (DPMS) helps raise awareness of the risks to the freedoms and rights of data subjects, thus avoiding fines and penalties.

    My work involves the design and support for the implementation of a company-specific DPMS, as well as assistance in maintaining compliance with the regulations set forth by EU Regulation 2016/679.

  • Cybersecurity

    Protecting a company's cyber perimeter is a crucial element in ensuring business security, continuity, and safeguarding its reputation.

    Implementing an efficient Cybersecurity Management System (CSMS) enhances the understanding of a company’s digital assets and increases awareness of related cybersecurity risks.

    My work focuses on designing and supporting the implementation of a customized CSMS tailored to the company, based on the structure of the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

  • Artificial Intelligence

    The AI Act is a proposed regulation by the European Union aimed at governing the use of Artificial Intelligence by classifying applications based on their level of risk and imposing stricter rules for high-risk AI technologies, to ensure security, transparency, and respect for fundamental rights.

    My work focuses on assisting companies wishing to adopt these technologies, helping them avoid configuration errors and non-compliant uses.

  • Business Modelling

    With the increasing adoption of automated digital services through technologies such as Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, and Embedded Systems in value creation, Decentralized Finance becomes essential for new digital business models.

    I encourage every entrepreneur to revisit their business models and carefully evaluate whether they can be reimagined according to De-Fi paradigms.

    Using standard design processes such as Canvas, my work focuses on assisting entrepreneurs in designing business models that are suited to the future challenges of the Digital Economy.

«Ontology of Esperanto» Research Project

The project «Ontology of Esperanto» is proposed as one of the first attempts to produce a formal representation of a non-formal language (natural language), based on the Semantic Web standards.

«An OWL Ontology of a Sumerian Grammar» Research Project

The outcomes of the project «An OWL Ontology of a Sumerian Grammar» demonstrate how it is possible to apply the technologies of the Semantic Web also to formlize the grammar of an ancient natural language.

«Dhi Qar Knowledge Base» Research Project

«Dhi Qar Knowledge Base» is the name of the software development project performed in 2007-2008, aimed to create an innovative Knowledge-driven System designed for cataloguing archaeological and epigraphic finds.
